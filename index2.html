what is replikr?


Do you ever feel as though too much of your time is taken up with monotonous & time-consuming processing of photographs? Do you wish you had more time for family & friends during busy periods like summer & Christmas? Wouldn’t it be great if you could give your processing to someone else to do, freeing up your time to do the more important things?

That’s where replikr comes in!

Imagine what work could be like:

  1. The Wedding Day: Photograph the happy couple and their friends & family during the day.
  2. Upload all of your raw photographs to our server - Your photos are now automatically backed up and safe!
  3. Put your feet up and enjoy a glass of champagne, while our highly skilled engineers streamline and optimise your photographs (removing duds, blinks, blurs etc and to your personal preferences).
  4. Showcase your newly processed photo thumbnails on a website feauturing your own branding that very evening at the reception!
  5. Enjoy increased sales as customers can select & pay for photographs they’d like there and then.
  6. Leave the final finishing touches to us, or finish up at home if you enjoy it. We can then send all purchases to a printers of your choice, or one of our high quality, preferred suppliers.
  7. All processed photos are safely stored & backed up, and readily available for purchase, leaving you with less stress and more time! More time for relaxing or even for generating more work!

This model will work for all types of photography, but weddings is a particularly great example.
